Simple Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti - 200g
  • Tomato Sauce - 1 cup
  • Garlic - 2 cloves (minced)
  • Fresh Basil - 5 leaves

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Saut√© garlic in olive oil until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Toss spaghetti with sauce and garnish with fresh basil.
